Tanzania

Arusha, located in northern Tanzania, is a vibrant city and a gateway to iconic attractions like Mount Kilimanjaro, Serengeti National Park, and Ngorongoro Crater. Known for its cool climate and bustling markets, it serves as a hub for safaris and cultural tourism, offering rich experiences in nature and local traditions.
